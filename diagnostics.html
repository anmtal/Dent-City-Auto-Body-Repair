Our Process

How the claim works

From the moment you call us to the day you pick up your keys, here's exactly how we move your insurance claim along.

1

Free estimate

Bring the vehicle in or send us photos. We assess the damage and write up a detailed estimate for your insurer — at no charge.

2

We file with your insurer

We submit the estimate, work with your adjuster on approvals and supplements, and confirm your coverage and deductible.

3

Repair & direct billing

Our technicians restore your vehicle with OEM-quality parts and factory-match paint, and we bill the insurer directly.

4

Back to brand-new

You collect your finished vehicle, detailed and inspected — backed by our lifetime warranty on workmanship.
